My Journey as an Entrepreneur

2025

When I began designing Normscape, it wasn’t just about business. It was about reimagining the boundaries of play. Drawing from my experience in immersive design and digital storytelling, I realized that modern escape rooms were missing a layer of personal connection. Normscape was born from the belief that every person deserves a break from normality, an intelligent, responsive space that adjusts to their curiosity.

A recent visitor, Alyssa (age 32), shared her story: “I walked into Normscape thinking it would be fun, but I walked out feeling like I had traveled to another dimension. Every puzzle felt like it knew me. I’ve done escape rooms all over the world, but this was unforgettable.”
